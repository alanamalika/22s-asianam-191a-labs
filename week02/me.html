About Me

Hi! I'm Alana. I am pursuing my B.A. in Global Studies with a minor in Digital Humanities from UCLA.
As an aspiring UX researcher, I am passionate about exploring the intersections of art, technology, and social change in creating user experiences that seamlessly reach beyond cultural and geographical barriers.
A people-oriented thinker, I extract concrete insight from raw data, empathetic solutions from human-centered research, and design inspiration from Southeast Asian art.
