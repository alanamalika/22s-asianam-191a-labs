Alana Malika's Map Portfolio

This project was created as partial completion of the course Asian American 191A: Web Development and GIS for Social Change. In this class, I hope to gain the technical skills to become a critical digital map maker. I grew up in a big metropolitan city, which has shaped the way I move throughout life. I have always been surrounded by cacophany and variety, so I am most comfortable being a small fish in a big pond. I thrive in searching for hidden gems, which translates into my passion in exploring people's relationship with space.I want to merge this passion with technical skills in GIS to highlight the unique issues within local communities and how it relates to space.
